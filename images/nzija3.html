Our Delivery Services

Motorcycle Express

Our fastest option with professional riders equipped with thermal bags for hot foods. Perfect for quick food deliveries across the city.

  • Max 15kg payload
  • 20-40 min delivery
  • GPS tracking

Eco Bike Delivery

Environmentally friendly option for shorter distances. Our bike riders are perfect for congested areas and short-distance deliveries.

  • Max 10kg payload
  • 30-50 min delivery
  • Zero emissions

Parcel Delivery

Secure document and package delivery with tamper-proof seals and real-time GPS tracking. Our AI optimizes routes for fastest delivery.

  • Tamper-proof packaging
  • Real-time tracking
  • Photo proof of delivery

Bulk Delivery

For larger orders, we coordinate multiple riders to ensure all your food arrives fresh and on time. Perfect for events or office catering.

  • No weight limit
  • Coordinated timing
  • Special packaging
